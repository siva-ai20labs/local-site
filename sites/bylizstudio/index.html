Meet the Artist

Lismary

ByLizstudio is the boutique studio of Lismary, a nail artist known for meticulous detail and genuinely warm client care. Every set is treated as a small canvas — hand-painted accents, considered color, and finishes that hold up beautifully.

It is an intimate, by-appointment space where you are never rushed. Whether you arrive with a clear vision or want to dream one up together, Lismary brings patience, precision, and a real love of the craft to every appointment.
